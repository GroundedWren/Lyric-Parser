Info

This subdomain is a tool to analyze song lyrics from discographies! It can be hard to keep track of running themes across all the years of releases from an artist, so having a place to poke through recurring words and phrases will hopefully prove helpufl.

To make sure variants of the same words are counted together, I'm using a Porter Stemmer tool, which is based on an algorithm originally described in 1980 to remove suffixes.

I'm also removing common words in English from analysis based on this list of stop words.

I've pre-built Iron & Wine's discography because I'm a huge fan and I wanted to see the analysis.
